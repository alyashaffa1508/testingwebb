About Us

UNJ Entrepreneur Club is a student organization at Universitas Negeri Jakarta dedicated to developing entrepreneurial skills and mindset. Providing opportunities to explore business ideas, enchance creativity and gain practical knowledge throough discussions, workshops, and collaborative projects.

Program Kerja

TEDxUNJ

Sebuah acara independen berlisensi TED yang diselenggarakan oleh Universitas Negeri Jakarta. Fokusnya menghadirkan gagasan inovatif dari akademisi, praktisi, dan pembicara muda untuk menginspirasi publik. Formatnya berupa talk berdurasi singkat dengan tema lintas disiplin.

International Business Strategy Competition

Kompetisi bisnis tingkat universitas yang menguji kemampuan analisis strategi, pemecahan masalah, dan presentasi peserta dalam menanggapi studi kasus bisnis internasional. Diikuti oleh mahasiswa dari berbagai kampus.

Founder Academy

Inisiatif inkubasi dan pelatihan startup bagi mahasiswa UNJ yang ingin membangun bisnis rintisan. Programnya mencakup pengembangan ide, validasi pasar, bimbingan mentor, networking, serta pembekalan manajemen bisnis modern.

Program Mahasiswa Wirausaha

Program pengembangan kewirausahaan yang dikelola UNJ untuk membantu mahasiswa membangun dan mengembangkan usaha. Menyediakan pelatihan, mentoring, serta pendanaan hibah bagi proposal bisnis terpilih.

Divisi Kami

Executive Board

The Executive Board serves as the central management of UEC, responsible for planning, organizing, managing, and evaluating all programs and activities to ensure they align with the organizations vision, mission, and overall objectives.

Marketing Strategist

The division that transforms ideas into impactful campaigns, blending creativity and strategy to make UEC’s presence stand out on every platform.

Program Development

The division that turns ideas into real experiences by planning, organizing, and executing impactful programs that bring the spirit of entrepreneurship to life.

Human Resources

The division that focuses on developing members’ potential, building strong teamwork, and creating a supportive environment where everyone can grow and thrive together.

Public Relation

The division that build and maintains UEC images and awareness by managing communications, collaborations and external relations to connect the organization with wider audience

Business Development

The division that builds partnerships, explores opportunities, and transforms creative ideas into impactful business solutions to support UEC programs.
